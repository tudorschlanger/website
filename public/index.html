I am a Research Assistant at the Bank of Canada where I work in the Model Development division of the Canadian Economic Analysis department.

I am passionate about monetary policy and macroeconomics and curious about the art of modelling to understand fundamental dynamics in the economy.

One of the reasons behind creating this website was to post about my interests, thoughts and ideas on a diverse array of topics, all united by a common economic flavour. I invite you to go to my blog and engage with these ideas in the comments section below the posts, or even share them!
